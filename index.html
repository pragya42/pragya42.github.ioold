Synthesis of Molybdenum Disulphide Quantum Dots
May 2018 – Jul 2018

Project description: Tasked to form Molybdenum disulfide Quantum Dots by liquid-phase exfoliation method

Approach

  • Prepared nanoparticles of Molybdenum disulfide by cryomilling using liquid nitrogen for 7 hours
  • Used X-ray diffraction and Scanning electron microscopy to confirm the formation of nanoparticles
  • Formed quantum dots through ultrasonication of MoS2 dispersed in solvent(DMF) for 20 hours
  • Verified the formation of quantum dots through Raman spectroscopy, UV Visible Spectroscopy, transmission electron microscopy, and Field emission scanning electron microscopy

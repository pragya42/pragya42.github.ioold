  1. The blade itself by Joe Abercrombie : Okay! It didn't rain like I thought it would, the weather was way too pleasant actually. But despite the sun, these morally gray characters are scary. Nothing is scarier than a perfectly written, well fleshed out terrible character, that could so possibly exist in the world. But it's not just the characters, the writing's beautiful, the pacing is awesome. It's so action-packed. Also, the prologue seems like a cold open for a very dark T.V. show, and I totally love it. I'm so going to binge this trilogy in 2022.
